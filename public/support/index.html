Voicely Support

Voicely records meeting audio on iPhone and transcribes it locally with WhisperKit/CoreML, helping you create searchable transcripts without requiring a cloud transcription service.

Getting Started

  1. Install Voicely on your iPhone.
  2. Open the app and allow microphone access when you want to record.
  3. Download or select a local transcription model when prompted.
  4. Record a meeting, then keep Voicely open while local transcription runs.
  5. Review the transcript, replay audio if needed, and choose what to keep, delete, export, or sync.

Local Transcription and Model Downloads

Voicely uses WhisperKit/CoreML models on iPhone. Transcription is local to the device, but the model file itself may need to be downloaded before first use or when you change models.

Model downloads can be large and may require Wi-Fi, battery power, free storage, and time to complete. A larger model may improve quality in some recordings but can take more storage and processing time.

iCloud and Sync

If iCloud/CloudKit sync is enabled and available, Voicely data may sync through your Apple iCloud account. Sync depends on iCloud settings, device storage, network connection, Apple account status, and Apple service availability.

If you do not want Voicely data to sync through iCloud, check iOS Settings for iCloud and app-level iCloud access.

Troubleshooting

Microphone permission

If recording does not start, open iOS Settings, find Voicely, and confirm microphone access is enabled. Also check that another app is not actively using the microphone.

Model not loaded

If transcription cannot begin, confirm the selected model has finished downloading and that your device has enough free storage. Try keeping Voicely open until the download or preparation step completes.

Poor transcript quality

Move closer to the speaker, reduce background noise, avoid covering the microphone, and choose the correct language setting. For difficult audio, a larger local model may produce better results but can take longer.

Storage or sync issues

Long recordings and local models can use significant storage. Delete recordings you no longer need, free device storage, check iCloud storage, and confirm your network connection if sync appears delayed.

Privacy

Voicely is designed around local-first transcription, no advertising tracking, and no selling of user data. Read the privacy policy for details about local storage, optional iCloud/CloudKit sync, model downloads, and microphone permission.
