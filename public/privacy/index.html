Voicely Privacy Policy

Voicely is a local-first iPhone transcription app for sensitive meeting audio. Its core transcription workflow is designed around recording, processing, and reviewing audio on your device.

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Overview

Voicely records audio that you choose to capture and transcribes it locally on iPhone using WhisperKit/CoreML. Voicely is not an advertising product, does not sell your data, and does not use advertising tracking.

Some features may still use the network. For example, Whisper/CoreML model downloads may require internet access, and your recordings, transcripts, or app data may sync through your Apple iCloud/CloudKit account if sync is enabled and available on your device.

Information Voicely Handles

  • Audio recordings you create in the app.
  • Transcripts, titles, timestamps, and related meeting metadata generated from or added to those recordings.
  • App settings such as transcription language, model selection, sync status, and retention choices.
  • Basic technical information needed by Apple platforms to provide app distribution, permissions, iCloud/CloudKit, crash reporting, or system services, subject to Apple settings and policies.

Microphone Permission

Voicely requests microphone permission so you can record audio. If you deny or disable microphone access, recording features will not work until permission is enabled again in iOS Settings.

Local Transcription and Storage

Voicely uses WhisperKit/CoreML to transcribe audio on your device. Audio recordings and transcripts are stored on your device unless you choose to delete, export, or sync them through services available on your device.

Voicely does not claim that every app activity is completely offline. Model downloads, iCloud/CloudKit sync, App Store services, device backup, or user-initiated sharing may involve network activity.

iCloud and CloudKit Sync

If iCloud/CloudKit sync is enabled and available, Voicely data may sync through your Apple iCloud account so your data can be available across your devices. Apple provides and controls iCloud/CloudKit infrastructure, account authentication, encryption, availability, and related settings.

You can manage iCloud availability and app-level iCloud access in iOS Settings. Sync behavior can also depend on device storage, network connection, Apple account status, and Apple service availability.

Data Sharing

Voicely does not sell your data and does not use advertising tracking. Voicely does not require sending meeting audio to a cloud transcription API to create a transcript.

Data may leave your device when you enable iCloud sync, download models, use Apple platform services, create backups, export or share transcripts, or otherwise use operating system sharing features.

Retention and Deletion

You control the recordings and transcripts you create in Voicely. Deleting items in the app removes them from local app storage. If iCloud sync is enabled, deletion and propagation across devices may depend on CloudKit sync timing and Apple account state.

Children

Voicely is intended for professional and personal productivity workflows. It is not directed to children and does not knowingly sell or use advertising tracking for children's personal information.

Changes

This policy may be updated as Voicely changes. The latest version will be posted on this page.

Contact and Support

For privacy questions or app support, use the Voicely support page on this website.
